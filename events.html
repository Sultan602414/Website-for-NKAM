FUNFAIR

The Event named FUNFAIR is hosted by NKAM.In this special event students are asked to have stalls of different food items.Moreover,there are many other fun activities in this event like "antakshari" , "ramp walk" and many more like this.Students are highly encouraged to take part in the activities because these events are arranged for the students to provide them some rest from the hectic routine of studies.As it names as FUNFAIR which means an outdoor event featuring games, exhibitions, stalls and other entertainment activities.This event usually happend during the 1st semester of Freshers.Here are some pictures of the most recent event FUNFAIR hosted by NKAM.

AHWAL-E-NKAM

Ahwal-e-NKAM is a cultural and entertainment event hosted by the NKAM society, for the students of the university. This event includes a variety of stage performances, such as dances, skits, songs, and other forms of live entertainment, all performed by the students themselves. The purpose of the event is to provide an opportunity for students to showcase their talents and skills, as well as to bring people together for a night of entertainment and cultural exchange. The event can also serve as a fundraiser for the society, if tickets are sold to attend the performance. Ahwal-e-NKAM can be a great way for students to bond, express themselves creatively, and connect with others who share similar interests. The event is also an opportunity for the society to showcase the rich cultural heritage and traditions of the university and its students, and to promote cultural awareness and appreciation among the members of the university community.

ANUAL PLAY

The annual play hosted by the NKAM society, is an event for the students and espacially forthe 4th year students. This event is a theatrical performance that includes a variety of stage performances, such as modelling, song performances, skits, and other forms of live entertainment. The purpose of the annual play is to provide an opportunity for students to showcase their talents and skills in a dramatic and theatrical setting, as well as to bring people together for a night of entertainment and cultural exchange. The event is typically held at the end of the year, as a way to celebrate the achievements of the students and the university community as a whole. The annual play can also serve as a fundraiser for the society, if tickets are sold to attend the performance. Overall, the annual play is a great way for students to express themselves creatively, bond with others who share similar interests, and end the academic year on a high note

THEME DINNER

Typically, this event is planned around the middle of December. In it, themes are given to the students according to which they have to wear the clothes, For example, last year's theme dinner had a black colour motif, and students were instructed to dress in accordance with those themes. However, the themes for the upcoming Theme Dinner are Sky (Blue), Earth (Brown), Mountain (Grey), and Forest (Green). It brings us a lot of delight.

MOVIE NIGHT

The purpose of the event is to provide a fun and social atmosphere for the members of the society, as well as to bring students together and foster a sense of community. The event can also serve as a fundraiser for the society, if tickets are sold to attend the movie night. The organizers may also arrange for snacks and drinks to be available during the movie, creating a cinema-like experience for the attendees. Overall, a movie night hosted by a university society can be a great opportunity to have a fun evening and connect with others who share similar interests. Students have the chance to see a great movie with their friends at this event on a huge screen (like theater). The movie selection is excellent, which will benefit the students by allowing them to take a break from their demanding study schedule.
